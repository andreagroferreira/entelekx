# EntelekX — Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. The Solo Founder

**Name:** Alex  
**Age:** 28–42  
**Context:** Building a startup alone or with one co-founder. Technical or semi-technical.

**Goals:**
- Ship an MVP fast.
- Automate marketing, sales and ops without hiring.
- Keep total cost low.
- Maintain focus instead of context-switching between 12 tools.

**Pain points:**
- Spends too much time wiring tools together.
- Cannot afford a designer, copywriter, or junior dev.
- Forgets decisions and context across projects.
- Worries about data ownership.

**What EntelekX gives Alex:**
- A dev studio that scaffolds, codes and deploys.
- A business brain that tracks goals, tasks, notes and decisions.
- A Fusion engine that gets high-quality answers from multiple models.
- A media forge for brand assets.

---

### 2. The Senior Developer / Indie Hacker

**Name:** João  
**Age:** 30–50  
**Context:** Experienced engineer, builds side projects, consults, or runs a small agency.

**Goals:**
- Move from idea to production with minimal friction.
- Own the stack end-to-end.
- Use the best model for each task.
- Keep code quality high without a team.

**Pain points:**
- Existing tools are too narrow (Copilot for code, ChatGPT for chat).
- Hard to maintain context across repos, clients and projects.
- Wants local-first but needs real power.

**What EntelekX gives João:**
- Multi-model access from one interface.
- Project-aware context and memory.
- Real code execution in a sandbox.
- Fast scaffolding for Laravel, Nuxt, Next.js, FastAPI and Node.

---

### 3. The Non-Technical Builder

**Name:** Maria  
**Age:** 25–45  
**Context:** Domain expert, creator, consultant or small business owner. Not a developer.

**Goals:**
- Build web apps, automations and content without learning to code.
- Understand what the AI is doing.
- Iterate on ideas visually.
- Delegate repetitive work to agents.

**Pain points:**
- No-code tools hit walls quickly.
- Feels dependent on freelancers or agencies.
- Existing AI tools assume technical fluency.

**What EntelekX gives Maria:**
- Natural-language scaffolding and design.
- Visual preview of apps and assets.
- Agent teammates she can instruct in plain language.
- Clear explanations of what the system is doing.

---

### 4. The Unique-Person Company

**Name:** Sam  
**Age:** 30–55  
**Context:** Runs a one-person business doing $10k–$100k/month. Wants to scale without employees.

**Goals:**
- Replace departments with agents.
- Make better strategic decisions.
- Produce content, product and operations at scale.
- Protect intellectual property and customer data.

**Pain points:**
- Tools are fragmented and subscription costs pile up.
- Hard to coordinate AI agents across functions.
- Lacks a single source of truth for the business.

**What EntelekX gives Sam:**
- A life and business OS.
- Department-like agent teams (sales, marketing, product, ops).
- A knowledge graph of the business.
- Self-hosted sovereignty.

## Anti-Personas

- Large enterprise teams looking for top-down IT governance first.
- Users who want a simple chatbot and nothing else.
- People unwilling to run software locally or on their own VPS.
