# EntelekX — Life OS

## Goal

Give the user a unified environment to manage their life and business: notes, tasks, calendar, goals, decisions and knowledge. This is the "manual of life" layer.

## Modules

### Notes

- Rich Markdown notes with AI-assisted editing.
- Backlinks and graph view.
- Tags and projects.
- Templates for meeting notes, ideas, research, etc.
- Export to PDF, HTML, DOCX.

### Tasks

- Task creation from chat.
- Projects, due dates, priorities, dependencies.
- Recurring tasks.
- Agent delegation: "@entelekx build this landing page" becomes a tracked task.
- Integration with calendar and goals.

### Calendar

- Local calendar view.
- CalDAV sync (future).
- Scheduled agent tasks.
- Reminders.

### Goals

- Goal hierarchy: long-term → quarterly → monthly → weekly.
- Milestones and progress tracking.
- Link tasks and decisions to goals.
- Agent check-ins and progress summaries.

### Decisions

- Decision log with context, options, reasoning and outcome.
- Retrospective prompts.
- Link to goals and notes.

### Knowledge Graph

- Entities: people, projects, ideas, concepts, documents.
- Relations between entities.
- Graph exploration UI.
- AI-generated summaries of connected ideas.

## AI Integration

- Ask questions across all notes.
- Summarise projects and weeks.
- Suggest next actions.
- Extract tasks and decisions from conversations.
- Generate weekly reviews and plans.

## Business "Departments"

EntelekX can spawn agent teams for:
- **Strategy**: market analysis, positioning, roadmaps.
- **Product**: feature specs, user stories, prioritisation.
- **Engineering**: scaffolding, coding, testing, deploy.
- **Marketing**: content, SEO, campaigns, decks.
- **Sales**: outreach, CRM notes, follow-ups.
- **Operations**: tasks, calendar, reminders, backups.

Each department is a collection of skills, tools and scheduled tasks, not a separate app.
