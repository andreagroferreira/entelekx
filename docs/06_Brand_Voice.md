# EntelekX — Brand Voice

## Name

**EntelekX**

Pronunciation: /en-TEL-ekks/  
Origin: "entelechy" (Aristotle) + "X" (multiplier, crossing point, exponential).

## Meaning

Entelechy is the realisation of potential. X is the unknown factor that multiplies it. EntelekX is the system that helps a single person become what they are capable of becoming — across thinking, building, deciding and creating.

## Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|---|---|
| **Confident but not arrogant** | We believe in the user's potential, not our own hype. |
| **Technical but clear** | We speak precisely, without unnecessary jargon. |
| **Empowering** | The tool exists to amplify the user, not replace them. |
| **Focused** | Every feature serves a purpose: think, build, decide, ship. |
| **Sovereign** | User data and choices come first. |

## Tone

- Direct, active voice.
- No AI-sounding filler ("As an AI language model...").
- Concrete examples over abstract claims.
- When explaining, start with the user's outcome.
- When warning, be clear and actionable.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Say: "EntelekX turns your machine into a personal AI company."
- Say: "Run a panel of models, then ship the result."
- Use plain language for complex ideas.
- Respect the user's intelligence and time.

### Don't
- Say: "AI will replace you." We replace friction, not people.
- Use hype words like "revolutionary" or "game-changing" without proof.
- Pretend to be human.
- Hide self-hosting complexity behind vague marketing.

## Visual Direction (Initial)

- Clean, high-contrast, utilitarian with moments of polish.
- Prefer system-aware light/dark modes.
- Typography: technical but readable (monospace accents, clean sans).
- Imagery: abstract networks, code, decision trees, workflow maps.

## Tagline Candidates

- "One person. Infinite output."
- "The OS for the one-person company."
- "Think, build, decide, ship — in one place."
- "Your context. Your models. Your ship."
